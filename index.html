How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Note: The plays are ordered chronically: Secret Love in the Land of the Peach Blossoms(暗恋桃花源), Thunderstorm(雷雨), The Lighter(打火机), Zou and the King(邹忌讽齐王纳谏), Waiting(等待), Love in a Fallen City(倾城之恋), 27, Train Station(火车站), Rhinoceros in Love(恋爱的犀牛), 12 Angry Men(十二人), I Myself He Himself(我和我和他和他), and finally, our current production Ruan Lingyu(阮玲玉)!
